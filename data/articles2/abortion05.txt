Worth. I&#8217;ve got 30 drivers (at my immediate plant), three front end loaders with operators, three mechanics, two guys (batch men) loading trucks, and two guys who maintain the yard. (I know you&#8217;re wondering what I do around the place &#8230; don&#8217;t worry about that. I get paid for what I know, not what I do. KIDDING!)</p> <p>The mechanics, loader operators and yard guys are all Mexican, all but one are related. (brothers, uncle, etc.) They are all pretty good workers and all-round very good folks. One of the yard men enrolled in Obama-care. He just shakes his head whenever he or his wife has to visit a doctor or have some medical procedure done. <em>He </em>tells <em>me </em>constantly how Obama-care isn&#8217;t worth the paper the plan is printed on and how frustrated he is at the expense and how meeting the deductible is near impossible, and if he does finally meet it, it&#8217;s at the year end and has to start over again. He has family (mother, siblings) in Mexico who he visits every year.</p> <p>Yesterday he told me that the United States needs a Republican president and that he would never vote Democrat. He&#8217;s a legal immigrant.</p> <p>~~~</p> <p>I remember last month Mike Huckabee said that Obama&#8217;s <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2015/07/26/politics/huckabee-obama-israel-oven-door/">nuclear agreement with Iran </a>is <em>&#8221; &#8230;  so naive that he would trust the Iranians. By doing so, he will take the Israelis and march them to the door of the oven.&#8221;</em></p> <p>Debbie Was-a-Man Schultz voiced her phony outrage at Huckabee&#8217;s comment with,  <em>&#8220;This rhetoric, while commonplace in today&#8217;s Republican presidential primary, has no place in American politics. Cavalier analogies to the Holocaust are unacceptable. Mike Huckabee must apologize to the Jewish community and to the American people for this grossly irresponsible statement.&#8221;</em></p> <p>Oh the indignation, the vexation and disgust that anyone would question Barack&#8217;s and John Kerry&#8217;s negotiating skills!</p> <p>Well, as &#8220;vexing&#8221; as Mr. Huckabee&#8217;s comments were (personally I think he is right on) you cannot outdo Barack. We&#8217;re all aware the Iranians are committed to the demise of America and Israel. Does the phrase &#8220;Death to America&#8221; ring a bell? Obama said that not <em>all </em>Iranians believe that. (I&#8217;m not sure what there is to &#8220;believe&#8221; but &#8230; who cares?) Anyway, <a href="http://downtrend.com/videos/cg8z6hxs1oy?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+downtrend+%28Downtrend.com%29">Obama said </a>that the &#8220;hardliners&#8221; who chant &#8220;Death to America&#8221; and oppose his deal are <em>&#8220;making common cause with the Republican Caucus.&#8221;  </em>I&#8217;m not sure what that&#8217;s supposed to mean. I&#8217;ll readily admit that Obama is so far above me intellectually, that he has to dumb-down his statements to my level of understanding. It just seems to me that he&#8217;s saying the Republicans are in the same category as the &#8220;Death to America&#8221; crowd. Odd I don&#8217;t see any outrage from the main-stream Obama butt kissing media. I guess whatever Obama and company says and does <em>has </em>a place in American politics.</p> <p>~~~</p> <p>What&#8217;s very cool right now is the fact that the Democrats have two serious candidates for the presidential nomination. One is under investigation by the FBI and has more baggage than Michelle Obama, her mother and kids going on a two week vacation.  The other is a 74 year old white guy who borders on being senile. Oh, I almost forgot that Joe Biden is considering running as well. I&#8217;m loving it. These folks are the best the Democrats have! <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elmer_Fudd">Elmer Fudd </a>(R &#8211; Loony Toons) could beat any of these folks easily, even with the major media covering for them.</p> <p>~~~</p> <p>The damning videos about Planned Parenthood make defending the organization ludicrous. Their acts are something like out of a <a href="http://www.netflix.com/title/582793">Hellraiser </a>or <a href="http://www.netflix.com/title/70098905">Saw</a> movie. This statement from a piece at <a href="http://www.examiner.com/article/fox-news-regular-defends-planned-parenthood-shuts-down-conservative-panel-guest">examiner.com </a>is about as lame and hollow-ringing as anything the left has to spew &#8230;  <em>Following in the footsteps of many before them, the Center for Medical Progress is yet another anti-abortion group whose sole purpose is to deny a woman the right to choose.</em></p> <p>Want to read some of the most asinine reasoning by liberals?
