itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Article" id="content" class="row" style="direction: rtl">  						  						<div id="main" class="col w100" style="direction: ltr"> 							     <h3 class="top_slug">INTRODUCTION</h3>  <h1 class="title"> 	<span class="title-gray">SUPPORTING GLOBAL</span><br /> 	<span class="campaign-color">HUMAN RIGHTS AWARENESS</span> </h1>  <div class="body"> 	<p>Early in his research into the human mind and spirit, author, humanitarian and Scientology Founder L. Ron Hubbard emphasized that spiritual freedom and enlightenment were all but unattainable goals to individuals denied their most fundamental human rights.</p>   	      <div class="pull-quote left campaign-color"> 	CENTRAL <span class="small-gray">to</span> SCIENTOLOGY BELIEFS <span class="small-gray">is a</span> CONVICTION <span class="small-gray">that all</span> HUMANKIND <span class="small-gray">is entitled to</span> INALIENABLE RIGHTS. 	</div>  	<p>Accordingly, he wrote the Code of a Scientologist, calling on all members of the religion to dedicate themselves “to support true humanitarian endeavors in the fields of human rights.” In doing so, he laid the groundwork for what has become a global movement of advocates for the human rights of all people, regardless of their social condition, ethnicity or religious affiliation. </p> 	<p>Today, Scientologists on five continents engage in collaborative efforts with government agencies and nongovernmental organizations to bring about broad-scale awareness and implementation of the 1948 United Nations Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the world&rsquo;s premier human rights document. </p> 	 <p>We welcome the participation and collaboration of all who share the goal of a world where human rights are an everyday reality for all.</p> 		   	<br /> </div>   <div class="index-sub-sections">  <div class="gray-divider-line"></div> <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/global-education.html">     <h1 class="title">         <span class="title-gray">EDUCATING PEOPLE ACROSS THE GLOBE</span><br />        <span class="campaign-color">ABOUT HUMAN RIGHTS</span>     </h1> </a>     <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/02_UN-aparteheid_boundaryfence1982_3X2.jpg" /> </div>  <div class="section-blurb"> 	<p>We are confronted daily with news of ethnic conflicts, inhumane practices, civil unrest and war. </p> <p>The media is filled with stories of human rights violations&mdash;from domestic violence and school bullying to corruption and poverty and government-sanctioned discrimination. All the while, a majority of the world&rsquo;s population does not even know what human rights are and that they are entitled to them. </p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/global-education.html">more</a> </span> <br /> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/multimedia-materials.html">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">MULTIMEDIA EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS</span><br/>     <span class="campaign-color">UNITED FOR HUMAN RIGHTS</span>     </h1> </a>     <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/03_30-Human-Rights-PSAs-in-school_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb"> 	<p>With the support of the Church of Scientology, multimedia human rights materials educate and raise awareness, reaching more than 100 million individuals worldwide each year.</p>     <p>The United Nations&rsquo; Universal Declaration of Human Rights became the specific educational focus of the program after surveys showed that less than 10 percent of the population knew of the existence of the Declaration and even fewer could name more than one or two of the 30 basic rights articulated in the document.</p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/multimedia-materials.html">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/voice-for-human-rights.html">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">A COMPELLING VOICE FOR HUMAN<br /> RIGHTS, INSPIRING MILLIONS</span><br />        <span class="campaign-color">&ldquo;30 RIGHTS, 30 ADS&rdquo;</span> 	</h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/05_Human-Rights-PSA-Zurich-Central-Station_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb">     <p>The increasing interest of young people in learning their human rights generated by the <em>What are Human Rights?</em> booklet led to the creation of 30 educational public service announcements (PSAs), each depicting one of the human rights laid out in the Universal Declaration.</p> <br /> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/voice-for-human-rights.html">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/audiovisual-elements.html">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">AUDIOVISUAL ELEMENTS TO BRING</span><br />     <span class="campaign-color">HUMAN RIGHTS TO LIFE</span> 	</h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/08_Youth-Human-Rights-Summit-Speaker_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb"> 	    <p>The film <em>The Story of Human Rights</em> is a key component of the human rights education campaign, introducing viewers of all ages to their most fundamental freedoms.</p>     <p><em>The Story of Human Rights</em> is a striking 10-minute documentary defining one of the world&rsquo;s most misunderstood subjects.</p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/audiovisual-elements.html">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/online-resource-center.html">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">ONLINE EDUCATION RESOURCE CENTER</span><br />      <span class="campaign-color">INTERACTIVE WEBSITE</span> 	</h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/14_United-for-Human-Rights-Website.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb"> 	<p>Both United for Human Rights and Youth for Human Rights International maintain interactive websites with the full array of resources available in 17 languages. </p> 	<p>All human rights booklets, PSAs and <em>The Story of Human Rights</em> are available to download or order online. The websites feature an interactive virtual educational system for anyone teaching or learning basic human rights.</p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/online-resource-center.html">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/community-actions.html#slide7">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">HUMAN RIGHTS EDUCATION</span><br />         <span class="campaign-color">ENCIRCLING THE GLOBE</span>     </h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/17_Buddhist-Monk-Signing-Human-Rights-Proclamation_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb">     <p>A basic premise of human rights is that they are for everyone. Thus, regardless of economic or cultural background, any individual, by participating in these programs, can galvanize popular support for human rights and human rights education in their own community, city or country.</p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/community-actions.html#slide7">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>   <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/solutions-to-millions.html">     <h1 class="title"> 				<span class="title-gray">BRINGING SOLUTIONS</span><br />         <span class="campaign-color">TO THE MILLIONS</span>     </h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/30_Bringing-Solutions-to-Millions_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb"> 	<p>The Church of Scientology International Dissemination and Distribution Center in Los Angeles, California, stands at the heart of the global humanitarian initiatives supported by Churches of Scientology and Scientologists. In addition to generating dissemination materials for the more than 10,000 Scientology Churches, Missions and affiliated groups worldwide, the center produces educational materials for all Church-sponsored humanitarian programs.</p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/solutions-to-millions.html">more</a> </span> <br> <div class="gray-divider-line"></div>    <a href="/how-we-help/human-rights/program-results.html?video=hr_ol">     <h1 class="title"> 		<span class="title-gray">PARTNERS AND LEADERS</span><br />     <span class="campaign-color">SUPPORT THE PROGRAM</span> 	</h1> </a> <div class="inline-image-200">     <img src="/sites/default/files/imagecache/gcui_inline_200/howwehelp/human-rights/32_International-Walk-Human-Rights-Denmark_3X2.jpg" /> </div> <div class="section-blurb"> 	<p>Achieving global implementation of human rights requires widespread human rights education. </p>  <p>To bring it about, for more than 40 years Churches of Scientology and their members have initiated and supported programs that effectively raise popular awareness of human rights and teach them to youth and adults. </p> </div> <span class="learn-more">     <a
