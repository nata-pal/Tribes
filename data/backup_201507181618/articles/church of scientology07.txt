Kids</strong> is designed, owned, and operated by <a title="Meet the Admins" href="http://exscientologykids.com/esk2.0/meet-the-admins/">three young women</a> who grew up in Scientology, and later left the Church. We feel that growing up in the Scientology environment is a unique experience that&#8217;s almost impossible to comprehend unless you&#8217;ve lived it yourself. For what it&#8217;s worth, we offer non-judgmental support for those who are still in Scientology, discussion and debate for those who&#8217;ve already left, and a plethora of easy-to-understand references for the curious.</p> <p><strong>For the record,</strong> while the site admins make every effort to be non-judgemental, that doesn&#8217;t mean we&#8217;re unbiased. Most of the people that write for this site have had extremely negative experiences in Scientology. Some of us have lost our families due to Scientology&#8217;s Disconnection Policy, some of us have experienced physical abuse, and some of us were denied a proper education. However, we do welcome pro-Scientology email, comments and forum posts. There&#8217;s even a &#8220;no registration required&#8221; forum area where anyone may anonymously post questions, comments and advice.</p> <hr /> <p><strong>Scientologists: </strong>We understand that you don&#8217;t want to ruin your case by accidentally looking at the OTIII materials, and we respect that. We&#8217;ve done our best to keep all info on confidential materials clearly marked and partitioned off from the rest of the site. If you want to look, look. If ya don&#8217;t, ya don&#8217;t have to.</p> 						<a href="http://exscientologykids.com/about/" class="readmore"><span>Read More</span></a> 					</div> <!-- end #about --> 							 					<div id="recent-posts"> 				<h3>Latest News</h3> 										<div class="r-post clearfix"> 															<div class="thumb"> 									<a href="http://exscientologykids.com/the-unbreakable-miss-lovely-how-the-church-of-scientology-tried-to-destroy-paulette-cooper/"> 										<img src="http://exscientologykids.com/esk2.0/wp-content/uploads/et_temp/Unbreakable-37729_60x60.jpg" class='r-post-image'  alt='The Unbreakable Miss Lovely: How the Church of Scientology tried to destroy Paulette Cooper' width='60' height='60' />										<span class="overlay"></span> 									</a> 								</div> 	<!-- end .thumb --> 														 							<p class="date">May 26, 2015 | </p> 							<h4 class="title"><a href="http://exscientologykids.com/the-unbreakable-miss-lovely-how-the-church-of-scientology-tried-to-destroy-paulette-cooper/">The Unbreakable Miss Lovely: How the Church of Scientology tried to destroy Paulette Cooper</a></h4> 							<p>In 1971 Paulette Cooper wrote a scathing book about the Church of Scientology.
