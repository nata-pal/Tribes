Test Here</strong></a></p> <p>The most widely used herbicide in the world is Roundup. Its active ingredient is glyphosate. Thirty five years ago, Monsanto had studies showing potential harm to humans. And for all those decades Monsanto has been telling us roundp.jpgRoundup was perfectly safe.</p> <p><br>About a month ago, the International Agency for Research On Cancer (IARC) of the World Health Organization came out with the announcement that they were reclassifying glyphosate to a 2A rating of “Probable Carcinogen”. According to Dr. Mae Wan Ho and Dr. Nancy Swanson, “... a classification of 2A in cancer-causing potential for glyphosate on the IARC is almost the highest possible categorization.” Read more here.</p> <p>The USDA has reported that glyphosate is in our water and rivers. Eighty percent of GMOs are “Roundup Resistant.” This means they are engineered to withstand multiple applications of roundup and still survive. NonGMO crops, such as wheat, sugar cane, legumes and more, use it as a desiccant to have crops “mature” all at the same time for ease of harvesting. It is sprayed on playgrounds, schoolyards, alongside roads, lawns, sidewalks. It's everywhere.</p> <p><br>Ever wonder if it's in your body? Or more importantly, your kids' bodies?</p> <p><br>For the first time ever, our movement gets to unite and do a crowd funded study on the prevalence of this probable carcinogen in our population. This project is being brought to us by "Feed The World" and Organic Consumers Association in the US.<br><br>What will testing do for us?</p> <ul> <li>Provide us personal information so we know what’s going on in our and our children’s bodies. If we know glyphosate is in our bodies we can do something about it.</li> <li>Provide data to have more ammo against Monsanto that will let the world know what they are really doing to us, not the stories their PR mill cranks out.</li> <li>Give us more ammo with our representatives to push them to ban glyphosate. </li> </ul> <p><a href="http://feedtheworld.info/glyphosate-testing-test-yourself/?ngo=LabelGMOs.org">Find out more about glyphosate testing and taking part in a historic study that will let you know if you have this probable carcinogen in your body. In addition, you’ll help humanity and the planet by contributing to the body of knowledge that will help us get this poison banned.</a></p> <p>Bit by bit we are uniting to create a safer, less toxic world for our kids and their kids. Please join us and be part of the solution! The study is crowd funded.
