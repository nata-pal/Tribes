.entry-header -->  	<div class="entry-content"> 		<div data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_4835" style="width: 203px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://scicrit.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/takedown.png"><img class="wp-image-4835 size-medium" src="https://scicrit.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/takedown.png?w=193&#038;h=300" alt="takedown" width="193" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Click on the image to see a larger version in a new tab</p></div> <p>I suppose this had to happen eventually.</p> <p>The cloud service Mega, which I use to provide downloads from this blog, have deleted 14 files from my account after a complaint from the  Church of Scientology that these are copyright material. These are all in the &#8220;Secret Scientology Texts and Media&#8217; Section.</p> <p><strong>Think about that: Scientology, which claims to be a religion, is so afraid of what the general public might think if they read about official Scientology doctrine and practices, almost always laid down in the founder&#8217;s own words, that they abuse copyright in order to prevent you reading about them</strong>.</p> <p>Shouldn&#8217;t Scientologists be trying to spread the word, not suppress it? It’s rather like Christians using the law to prevent non-believers learning about the sermon on the mount, or the ten commandments. What <em>is</em> the Church of Scientology afraid of?</p> <p>However, as L Ron Hubbard himself said, complaints are a sign of success &#8211; so I am encouraged that this blog is making a difference. After the break, there is a list of the areas which I now know that the Church of Scientology is very afraid of the general public becoming aware of. <span id="more-4834"></span></p> <p><strong>Edit</strong> <strong><a title="New Links to Scientology Doctrines that the Church is *so* Ashamed of That it Tries to Suppress Them" href="https://scicrit.wordpress.com/2015/04/24/new-links-to-scientology-doctrines-that-the-church-is-so-ashamed-of-that-it-tries-to-suppress-them/">The files affected are now available again, via new links in this post</a> </strong></p> <p>The affected files include:</p> <ul> <li>Scientology&#8217;s Security Checklists (including the notorious Johannesburg &#8216;confessional&#8217; and the abusive Children&#8217;s security check)</li> <li>The Blue, Green and Red volumes</li> <li>OT (operating thetan) materials &#8211; including the OT3 level, which contains the &#8216;Xenu&#8217; story. This can surely look after itself &#8211; Hubbard claimed that anyone reading this material with out proper preparation by he Church of Scientology would die of pneumonia.</li> <li>An audio recording of Hubbard, in which he describes &#8220;The wall of fire&#8221; through which he had to pass to learn the secrets of OT3</li> <li>&#8216;Revolt in the Stars&#8217; a screenplay that Hubbard wrote which, incredibly uses the OT3 &#8216;Xenu&#8217; story that is supposed to kill&#8230;</li> <li>Scientology&#8217;s &#8216;Assists&#8217; handbook, which describes Scientology&#8217;s form of &#8216;healing hands&#8217; &#8211; including the tragicomic &#8220;bring back to life assist which consists of shouting &#8220;get back into the body&#8221; at a dead person.</li> <li>&#8216;The Hard Sell Pack&#8217; a compilation of Hubbard&#8217;s remarks which teaches Scientologists to sell Scientology services in the same aggressive way as used cars</li> <li>A Sea Org &#8216;training manual&#8217;</li> </ul> <p>Please be assured, I still have copies of all the affected files and the links will be restored as soon as find a way of storing them online which will not be subject to take-down (suggestions are most welcome).</p> <p><a href="https://scicrit.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/mimi-and-eunice-on-copyright.png"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-4836 size-full"
