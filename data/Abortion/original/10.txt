    <p><span id="more-74049"></span>Recently, Yahoo Lifestyle UK published <a href="https://uk.lifestyle.yahoo.com/powerful-video-shows-women-aged-15-50-describe-abortion-in-one-word-101158546.html">a piece</a> to do with a video from <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=beRWmz_7kOc">Cut Video. </a> This video featured women, ages 15-50, reacting to the word &#8220;abortion.&#8221; While there were some pro-life views, including from the two youngest women, many referred to an abortion as &#8220;a right,&#8221; or &#8220;a choice.&#8221; Some even referred to it as &#8220;necessary.&#8221;</p><div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;"><div id="uds-ads-34" class="widget-3 widget-odd postadvert hidden-sm hidden-xs widget widget_uds-ads"><p><span class='more-2'><a href='#' class='more'>ADVERTISEMENT</a></span></p><div id="uds-ads--1" class="uds-ads  uds-ads-position-2"><div class="uds-ads-251 uds-ad even"><a onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.lifenews.com/index.php?uds_ads_id=8');" href="http://www.lifenews.com/index.php?uds_ads_id=8" target="_blank"><img src="http://lifenews.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/americaneedsfatima5.jpg" alt="Ad Row 1"/></a></div><div class="clear"></div></div></div> <div id="uds-ads-22" class="widget-4 widget-even postadvert hidden-sm hidden-xs widget widget_uds-ads"><p><span class='more-2'><a href='#' class='more'>ADVERTISEMENT</a></span></p><div id="uds-ads--1" class="uds-ads  uds-ads-position-4"><div class="uds-ads-251 uds-ad even"><a onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.lifenews.com/index.php?uds_ads_id=13');" href="http://www.lifenews.com/index.php?uds_ads_id=13" target="_blank"><img src="http://lifenews.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/herchoicewebad3.jpg" alt="Not in use"/></a></div><div class="clear"></div></div></div></div> <p>Yahoo may have attempted to take a neutral view on the subject:</p> <p><em>Some people think it’s a personal choice while others steadfastly believe that it’s not acceptable – as this video by </em><em><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=beRWmz_7kOc">Cut</a> clearly shows.</em></p> <p>The heartfelt clip shows women aged between 15 and 50 describe their thoughts on abortion in one word, and there’s a huge mix of responses.</p> <p>Some of the women interviewed and entirely unaccepting of abortion and use strong words such as ‘killing’ to ‘horrible’ to describe it while others say it’s ‘necessary’ and a ‘right’.</p> <p>The piece does not point out that the reactions in favor of abortion outweighed those against it, and referred to the pro-life reactions as being expressed by &#8220;strong words.&#8221;</p> <p>The end of Yahoo&#8217;s reporting throws in a pro-abortion comment, and even links to a story of a woman who aborted her baby at eight months:</p> <p><em>Every woman who has an abortion has her own reason for doing it – as </em><em><a href="https://uk.lifestyle.yahoo.com/-what-kind-of-mother-is-8-months-pregnant-and-wants-an-abortion--080124682.html">one woman who   had an abortion at eight months pregnant knows only too well</a>. </em></p> <p>The piece also links to websites which may also attempt to portray a neutral viewpoint on abortion, but do support the procedure, and link to pro-abortion organizations in the United Kingdom, such as BPAS and Marie Stopes International, for those interested in &#8220;Post-abortion counselling.&#8221;:</p> <p><em>If you want advice, support or information about abortions, you can get in touch with </em><em><a href="http://www.abortionchoices.org/%20">Abortion Choices</a> or look at your choices on the <a href="http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/Abortion/Pages/Introduction.aspx">NHS website</a>.</em></p> <p>The responses include seven which are decidedly pro-life, including one woman at 35 who is not only against abortion, but who was asked to have an abortion, but did not, &#8220;and is really happy about it.&#8221; One of those responses, however, came from a 46 year old, who, while acknowledging that abortion is &#8220;killing,&#8221; said &#8220;depending on the situation, it could be okay.&#8221; It is also noteworthy that the two youngest women, ages 15 and 16, were both pro-life, which is reflective of the younger generations being pro-life.</p> <p><strong><a href="http://www.mailermailer.com/u/signup/1056801L">Click here to sign up for daily pro-life news alerts from LifeNews.com</a></strong></p> <p>There were also seven responses which, although referring to abortion in a negative light, did not take as much of a definitively pro-life stance against the procedure. Such responses included reacting to abortion as &#8220;scary&#8221; (24 year old), &#8220;touchy topic&#8221; and &#8220;hurtful&#8221; (28 year old), &#8220;pain,&#8221; no matter what decision the woman makes (31 year old),  &#8220;a hard decision&#8221; (33 year old), &#8220;confusing&#8221; (43 year old), &#8220;very sad,&#8221; (47 year old), and &#8220;unfortunate,&#8221; as well as the view that people are too careless with their sexuality, which is why there are abortions (50 year old). The 29 year old referred to abortion as &#8220;controversial,&#8221; which seems to take on a neutral connotation.</p> <p>That leaves 21 responses in favor of abortion. This includes 10 responses of &#8220;choice&#8221; or &#8220;pro-choice,&#8221; though the 26 year old acknowledged that &#8220;it needs to be handled very carefully and not lightly.&#8221; The 45 year old mentioned that she had originally been against abortion, but came to favor it over time. The 32 year old even says that it&#8217;s &#8220;her choice, at one week or 20 weeks.&#8221; Four women referred to abortion as &#8220;a right&#8221; or &#8220;a woman&#8217;s right.&#8221; Two women referred to abortion as &#8220;necessary&#8221; (19 year old and 40 year old). And two referred to it as &#8220;to each its own.&#8221; (30 year old and 44 year old). The 27 year old referred to it as &#8220;okay,&#8221; and the 34 year old responded with &#8220;none of your business.&#8221;</p> <p>The video showed the seventeen year old unable to come up with an answer.</p> <p><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/beRWmz_7kOc" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p> <p><a
