The Massive Shockwaves of Abortion

August 7, 2015 by FrBenCameron

Abortion affects millions of people in the United States, and hundreds of millions of people around the world.  It’s not only the women who have had abortions, but the fathers, grandparents, and siblings of those who have had abortions are affected.

Fr. Ben Cameron speaks of these “shockwaves of abortion.”  Have you been touched by abortion?

If you, or someone you love, has been wounded by abortion, we invite you to join us for a Rachel’s Vineyard retreat for post-abortion healing.  More information on our next retreat and our contact information are at the bottom of this page.
