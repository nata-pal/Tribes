Tom Cruise Reportedly Hasn't Seen Daughter Suri In Two Years Despite Being 'Blocks Away' From Her In NYC

Noelle Talmon
8/7/2015 10:03am EDT
Comment

Tom Cruise reportedly spent some time in New York City recently and didn't visit his daughter Suri. The actor allegedly hasn't seen her in two years, and they were last photographed publicly in 2013 during a vacation in New York's Adirondack Mountains.

An insider told In Touch that Cruise "was just blocks away from Suri and he didn't spend a moment of time with her."

A "friend" added, "That's the cold, harsh reality." 

On July 27, nine-year-old Suri and her mother Katie Holmes were photographed in the Big Apple while Cruise was taping "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" at 30 Rockefeller Center.

A source revealed: "There was no connection with Suri that day."

Cruise spent three days in the city to promote "Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation." According to the magazine, the actor and Suri never got together and he's missed some special moments in her life, such as her first day of school.

His friend explained, "These are important moments in Suri’s life, and Tom wasn’t there for them."

Suri is a spitting image of her parents

Another insider reported that the media was not allowed to ask him about his personal life while he was promoting his new film: "Tom will never address the Suri situation. It's not going to happen because it’s a no-win situation for him."

It's been rumored, but never confirmed, that Cruise's ties to Scientology have effectively ended his relationship with his daughter.  Representatives for the Church insist Holmes and her young daughter have not been deemed "suppressive persons." If they were, Cruise would not have any contact with them.

Cruise's representatives have said in the past that he does see his daughter -- in private and away from photographers.
