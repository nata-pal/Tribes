homophobia to racism, saying that both come from the same mindset.</p> <p style="margin-bottom: 0in">Speaking to a Young African Leaders Initiative audience on Monday in Washington, D.C., Obama was asked by a participant from Kenya about reports of people with albinism being killed and their body parts harvested for rituals.</p> <p style="margin-bottom: 0in">The president condemned the violence.</p> <p style="margin-bottom: 0in">“The same, by the way, is true of sexual orientation,” Obama said.  “I spoke about this in Africa, and everybody is like, 'Oh, oh, we don't want to hear
