  <span itemprop="datePublished" content="2015-08-06">                                 August 6, 2015</span> in <a itemprop="about" href="i_games.htm">Games</a></p>                         <p itemprop="articleBody">                             [prMac.com]                            Berlin, Germany - Screen Implosion announces the impending release of Downhill* Chapter One for the iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, an endless free fall on a minimal landscape with sparkling hidden surprises. With extraordinary graphics, and addictive gameplay, Downhill* will make players come back for more. Fun for everyone, everywhere, every time.<br /> <br /> Features:<br /> * Beautiful Architecture Graphics<br /> * 8 different Avant-Garde Tunes<br /> * Fast &amp; Smooth Scrolling<br /> * Special Night Stage<br /> * Game Center Support<br /> * Easter Eggs!<br /> <br /> The game is ready to launch in early September. It will be available for free in the App Store for the iPhone, iPod touch and iPad. It will feature a compelling gameplay based on speed and reflexes, also contemporary mechanics on the go, like cloning the avatar itself, and procedural creation of geometry. The landscape and overall concept of the game is based on minimalism.<br /> <br /> Players can compare their scores within Game Center or Google Play services, also via Twitter. Downhill* offers some surprises: There is a night stage with dedicated score table. Every few games the lights turn off and the setting looks completely different. Who has the skills to reach a high score at night?<br /> <br /> For more Downhill* Chapter One news and a first look at the trailer, please visit our Facebook page, and the Screen Implosion website. Follow us on twitter at: @screenimplosion.                        </p>                         <div class="para"><a href="http://www.screenimplosion.com" target="_blank" title="Screen Implosion" onClick="handleClick('1');">Screen Implosion</a><br /><a href="http://screenimplosion.com/index.php/downhill/" target="_blank" title="Downhill* Chapter One" onClick="handleClick('2');">Downhill* Chapter One</a><br /><a href="https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/4351226/splash_advertising_Downhill_coming_soon.png" target="_blank" title="Screenshot" onClick="handleClick('3');">Screenshot</a><br /><a href="http://screenimplosion.com/presskit/sheet.php?p=downhill" target="_blank" title="Media Assets" onClick="handleClick('4');">Media Assets</a><br /></div>                         <p>Screen Implosion is an independent developer founded in 2015 in Berlin who strives to produce classic arcade games with a contemporary twist.
