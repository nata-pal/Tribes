Iranian-born senior White House Advisor Valerie Jarrett who has a mysterious and dangerous hold on Obama (who <a href="http://cnsnews.com/news/article/israeli-paper-obama-adviser-valerie-jarrett-holding-secret-talks-iran"target=_blank"">held secret talks</a> with representatives of Iran's supreme leader Ayatollah Ali Khamene and is the <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2012/09/02/us/politics/valerie-jarrett-is-the-other-power-in-the-west-wing.html?pagewanted=print&_r=0"target=_blank"">mine behind a number of controversial Obama Administration policy decisions</a>), and Huma Abedin, who is an extremely close associate and friend of former Secretary of State and presidential candidate Hillary Clinton. World Trade Center bombing prosecutor Andrew McCarthy wrote in National Review: "The ties of Ms. Abedin's father, mother and brother to the Muslim Brotherhood are both specific and substantiated."</p>  <p>The Muslim Brotherhood took power in Egypt with the Obama administration's approval after it had all but abandoned the government of Hosni Mubarak, a long-time ally and friend.
