14 Augest<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/independence-day-of-pakistan-14-augest/happy_independence_day_independence_day_30/" rel="attachment wp-att-1226"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1226" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/Happy_Independence_Day_Independence_Day_30.jpg" alt="Happy_Independence_Day_Independence_Day_30" width="850" height="315" /></a> </strong>In This article we are telling you abuout Pakistan’s Independence Day, which is annually held on August 14, celebrates the country’s independence from the British rule on that date in 1947. This day is an occasion to promote patriotism and national unity.In This Independency day all the Pakistani get up early in the morning and go to mosque for prayer.<span id="more-1223"></span><a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-31.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1260" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-31.jpg" alt="images (31)" width="329" height="398" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">On this day Many people dress in or use the colors green and white, which are Pakistan’s official colors, during Pakistan&#8217;s Independence Day.On this day  all schools and colleges held different function .People all over Pakistan celebrate Independence Day with loyal zest. Many people who join the Independence Day processions dress up in green and white, which are the Pakistani flag’s colors. People visit national memorials and places of national rank to celebrate Independence Day. This is also a time to meet relatives, exchange gifts and visit recreational spots.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/independence-day-of-pakistan-14-augest/images-12/" rel="attachment wp-att-1241"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1241" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-12.jpg" alt="images (12)" width="334" height="322" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">On this day we decorate our homes,schools,colleges offices and streets with Pakistani flags.Pakistan’s national flag is also  hoisted on public, private buildings, residences and memorials at dawn. Official buildings, including Parliament House, are decorated and colorfully lit. The prime minister and president address and congratulate the nation and talk about the day’s significance.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-19.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1248" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-19.jpg" alt="images (19)" width="403" height="228" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">A change of guard takes place at national monuments, including the mausoleums of Muhammad Iqbal, who was a poet, philosopher, and politician, and Muhammad Ali Jinnah, who is considered by many as Pakistan’s founder and Quaid-i-Azam .Various Events held during this day include extraordinary theatre shows, national song competitions, concerts, and Independence Day pageants in all over Pakistan. Radio and television stations broadcast national songs and specially prepared Independence Day programs.On this Independence Day is an official holiday in Pakistan on August 14.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-14.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1243" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-14.jpg" alt="images (14)" width="349" height="238" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">All governmental, non-governmental offices, organizations, educational institutions, post offices, and major markets are closed in all over Pakistan.The Indo-Pak subcontinent remained a British colony from 1849–1947. The subcontinent’s populations did not approve of the British rule in united India. An rebellion that happened in 1857 eventually formed the shape of a freedom movement, which demonstrates the efforts of the people of British India in gaining their independence.<img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1239" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-10.jpg" alt="images (10)" width="310" height="236" /></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">Muslims ruled the subcontinent before the British intrusion and suffered due to the 1857 uprising against the British East India Company. Leaders such as Sir Syed Ahmed Khan and Syed Ameer Ali worked diligently for the Muslim people’s political advancement.The Indian National Congress was formed in 1885, which later on became British India’s largest political party. Major Muslim political leaders, including Muhammad Ali Jinnah, were members of the Indian National Congress and worked towards Hindu-Muslim unity and India’s independence.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1231" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-2.jpg" alt="images (2)" width="383" height="225" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">The All India Muslim League was founded on December 30, 1906. There was growing demand for an independent Muslim state in India. In 1930 Muhammad Iqbal presented the idea of a separate Muslim state that would geographically consist of the Muslim majority areas in the subcontinent. The word Pakistan is a conjunction of two words Pak meaning Pure and Stan meaning Land, a name coined by Choudhary  Rahmat  Ali.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-29.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1258" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-29.jpg" alt="images (29)" width="367" height="229" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">Tough Hindu nationalism gave birth to the Two Nation Theory, an ideology that is accredited as being the basis for Pakistan’s creation. In 1940 the Pakistan Resolution was passed in Lahore demanding greater Muslim autonomy in British India.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-30.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1259" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-30.jpg" alt="images (30)" width="382" height="171" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">After World War II, it was likely that the British colonies would soon disintegrate. The Pakistan Movement, directed by Muhammad Ali Jinnah, resulted in creating a Muslim state. Pakistan gained independence from the British rule on August 14, 1947.<a href="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-28.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1257" src="http://newstyleworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/images-28.jpg" alt="images (28)" width="380" height="288" /></a></p> <p style="text-align: justify;">At its birth, Pakistan had two parts commonly referred to as East and West Pakistan. In 1971 East Pakistan parted from West Pakistan and became Bangladesh.In the end we can say that Independence day is one of the most important day for Pakistani people and muslims Ummah all over the
