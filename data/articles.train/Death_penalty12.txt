Holmes to death has reached a verdict.</p> <p>The decision is expected to be read at 5 p.m. (7 p.m. CT).</p> <p><em><strong>**Channel 13 will have live coverage when his sentence is announced**</strong></em></p> <p>It is the same jury that convicted Holmes of 24 counts of first-degree murder and 140 counts of attempted murder last month in the July 20, 2012, massacre at a midnight screening of &#8220;The Dark Knight Rises&#8221; in Aurora. The jury deliberated less than 13 hours before reaching that decision.</p> <p>Since then, the jury has moved quickly through two prior decisions in the trial&#8217;s penalty phase, taking a few hours to vote that there were substantial &#8220;aggravating factors&#8221; to consider the death penalty, and that the defense&#8217;s arguments against execution didn&#8217;t outweigh those factors.</p>
